## What it does

- **Submit to HMRC** — quarterly updates, annual adjustments (BSAS) and the Final
  Declaration that replaces the old Self Assessment return, sent directly to HMRC.
- **All your income sources** — self-employment, UK property and foreign property,
  with consolidated or fully itemised expenses.
- **Year-end** — employment/PAYE (including P60 and P11D), dividends, pensions,
  Gift Aid and Marriage Allowance, brought together for crystallisation.
- **CIS** — view the Construction Industry Scheme deductions contractors have
  reported against you.
- **For accountants** — connect your Agent Services Account, invite and manage
  clients, and file for all of them from one place.
- **Import/Export** — bring figures in from Excel and CSV.
- **Private by design** — your figures, National Insurance number and UTR stay
  **encrypted on your own computer**. Only the submissions you choose are sent to
  HMRC — never to us.

## Getting started

1. **Download** the build for your platform above and open it.
2. **Enter your licence key** (from your [ThisQuarter account](https://thisquarter.co.uk/dashboard)) to activate.
3. **Connect to HMRC** — sign in with your Agent Services Account (accountants) or
   Government Gateway (individuals) on HMRC's own pages.
4. Add your income sources and submit.

New here? Sole traders start **free for their first two quarterly submission cycles** —
[create an account](https://thisquarter.co.uk/signup).

## Requirements

- macOS 12+, Windows 10/11 (64-bit), or a 64-bit Linux desktop.
- An internet connection to submit to HMRC and to validate your licence.
- An HMRC account with Making Tax Digital for Income Tax access.
